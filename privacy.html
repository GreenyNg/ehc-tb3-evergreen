Privacy Policy

Stadium is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect and use your personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you place an order:

  • Name
  • Address
  • Email
  • Phone number
  • Credit card information (which is securely processed and stored by our payment provider)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process your orders
  • Contact you regarding your orders
  • Improve our products and services

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your information with third parties except:

  • When necessary to process your order (such as with our payment provider)
  • When required by law

Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your personal information, including using secure servers and SSL encryption for all data transfers.

Updates to this Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The current version will be posted on our website.
